Notes and Thoughts

How opinionated do I want to be. The question struck me odd because this is a wireframe framework. So in a way very opinionated but biased to dirt simple. But this is an HTML/CSS wireframe, and by it's very design I want to show some basic interactions.

I want to love ems, I really do, but sometimes I need a 1px line. For wireframes, being off by 1px is driving me insane.

Some thing always feels wrong about empty tags..

UTF-8 automatically turning into emojis is both nice, and weird. I wish this was controllable by CSS. There's a weird hack where you append ︎ directly after.

Do you make the most common cast easy, but override all other occurrences? Or specify the common case to prevent scoping issues, but reduces the ease? "Every case is different", so what's the criteria by which you resolve this?

Every time I don't transition:all, I end up regretting it.

Where the hell are all the cool attr() stuff?

Why in the hell can't you style non-inline SVGs with CSS?!
